Lunch

Whole Wheat Chapati with Dal and Vegetable Curry

Serve 2-3 chapatis made from whole wheat flour alongside a bowl of dal (lentil soup) and a small bowl of mixed vegetable curry. This meal is rich in fiber, protein, and complex carbohydrates, helping to stabilize blood sugar levels

Brown Rice Pulao with Mixed Vegetables

A small bowl of brown rice pulao cooked with a variety of non-starchy vegetables like carrots, beans, and peas. Pair it with a side salad of cucumber and tomatoes for added fiber and nutrients

Quinoa Salad with Chickpeas

A refreshing salad made with cooked quinoa, chickpeas, diced cucumbers, tomatoes, onions, and a squeeze of lemon juice. This dish is high in protein and fiber, promoting satiety and better blood sugar control
