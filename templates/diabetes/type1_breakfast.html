Vegetable Upma

A savory dish made from semolina cooked with mixed vegetables like carrots, peas, and beans, seasoned with mustard seeds and curry leaves. This high-fiber meal helps maintain steady blood sugar levels

Moong Dal Chilla

Thin pancakes made from ground moong dal (split yellow lentils) mixed with spices and chopped vegetables. Rich in protein and fiber, these chillas are a nutritious start to the day

Oats Porridge with Nuts

Cooked oats topped with a mix of nuts (like almonds and walnuts) and seeds (like chia or flaxseeds), along with a sprinkle of cinnamon. This meal is high in fiber and healthy fats, promoting satiety
